About This Project

This project is a cloud-based International Student Support Website hosted on a Microsoft Azure Linux virtual machine. The website is designed to provide simple and useful information for international students who are adjusting to life and study in a new country.

The server was manually configured using SSH and command-line tools. NGINX is used as the web server, the domain name is connected using DNS, and HTTPS is enabled using a Let's Encrypt SSL/TLS certificate.

Living Expenses

Living expenses are one of the main challenges for international students. Students need to plan for rent, groceries, transport, phone bills, internet, and other daily costs.

  • Create a simple weekly or monthly budget.
  • Compare grocery prices before shopping.
  • Use student discounts where possible.
  • Keep some emergency savings for unexpected costs.

Study Tips

Studying in a new academic environment can take time. Students should stay organised and ask for support early if they are unsure about assessments or unit requirements.

  • Read the unit guide carefully.
  • Start assignments early.
  • Attend tutorials and labs regularly.
  • Keep notes from lectures and practical sessions.

Work and Visa Guidance

Many international students work part-time while studying. It is important to understand visa conditions and balance work with study responsibilities.

  • Check visa conditions using official government sources.
  • Keep records of payslips and work hours.
  • Balance work commitments with study workload.
  • Ask for support if workplace rights are unclear.

Tips and Tricks

Moving to a new country can be confusing at first, but small steps can make student life easier.

  • Save important contacts such as university support and emergency services.
  • Use public transport apps to plan travel.
  • Keep digital copies of important documents.
  • Join student groups to meet people and get support.
