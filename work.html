Working While Studying

Many international students work part-time while studying. Part-time work can help students manage living expenses, gain experience, and build confidence in a new country.

Important Things to Remember

  • Check current visa conditions using official government sources.
  • Make sure work hours do not affect study performance.
  • Keep payslips and work records safely.
  • Understand basic workplace rights and responsibilities.
  • Ask for help if something at work feels unclear or unfair.

Balancing Work and Study

Students should avoid taking too many work shifts during busy assessment periods. It is important to manage time carefully so that work does not affect assignment deadlines, attendance, or exam preparation.

This page provides general student guidance only. Students should always check official visa and government websites for current rules and requirements.
